Kino

Rock

One of the most popular Soviet rock bands of the 1980s, it was part of the Leningrad Rock Club. The leader and author of almost all lyrics and music has invariably remained Viktor Tsoi, after whose death the collective, which released more than a hundred songs on studio albums in nine years, several collections and live recordings, as well as a large number of unofficial bootlegs.
